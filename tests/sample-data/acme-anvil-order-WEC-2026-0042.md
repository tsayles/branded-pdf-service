### Performance Characteristics

The 200 lb configuration delivers a terminal velocity potential of
approximately 55 mph (88 km/h) from a standard two-story release height,
generating up to 1,200 ft·lbf (1,627 J) of impact energy. The wide base
flange provides stability on cliff ledges, canyon rims, and suspended
rope platforms.

The induction-hardened face resists deformation under repeated use,
while the soft-iron body absorbs and redistributes shock energy to
minimize rebound — a critical safety feature for operator proximity
scenarios.
